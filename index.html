Journal Short Film

Synopsis: Journal is an experimental short film about Arjun and Keerthana who are the new co-heads of a college photography magazine. Arjun, who is dominating and carefree in nature is insecure over the fact that Keerthana, who is insecure and silent in nature is made the final decision-maker among them, and his works are being questioned and criticized by her. The story shows how simple insecurity can create such turmoil between relationships and how things are patched up between them.

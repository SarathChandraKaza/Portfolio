About

I am a final year undergrad student who's passionate about telling stories through films. I believe filmmaking is one of the greatest storytelling techniques which can change our perspective of looking at the world and make us more empathetic. I primarily write, direct and edit but always ready to collaborate with other talented people.

Do check out my works, each one born out of deeply rooted personal stories around me. I'd always love to hear it from you all about how you felt after watching these. Have a nice day...
